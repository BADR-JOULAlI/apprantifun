Rainbow in the Sky

One rainy morning, little Sarah sat by her window, watching the raindrops fall. She was feeling a bit sad because she couldn't go outside to play.

"I wish there was something magical about rainy days," she sighed.

Suddenly, the rain began to slow down, and a bright ray of sunlight peeked through the clouds. Sarah noticed something amazing starting to appear in the sky.

"Look! A rainbow is forming!"

The rainbow stretched across the sky in a beautiful arc. Sarah's grandmother explained that rainbows have seven special colors:

Red like roses
Orange like sunset
Yellow like sunflowers
Green like grass
Blue like the ocean
Indigo like twilight
Violet like lavender

Sarah ran outside with her friends to look at the rainbow. They learned that rainbows appear when sunlight meets raindrops in the air - it's like nature's own magic show!

"Now I know why rainy days are special," Sarah smiled. "They bring us rainbows!"

That afternoon, Sarah and her friends drew pictures of the rainbow and all the colorful things it reminded them of. They learned that even on cloudy days, there's always a chance for something beautiful to appear.

Remember: After every rain comes a rainbow!
